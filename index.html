About

  • Marianne McGrath
  • Kenmare, Ireland
  • Fashion & Design diploma

My grandmother started me off in crafts when I was younger. I gained my diploma in 1997 and I have a working background in fashion design.

The work I do is inspired by the striking seasonal colours we experience in my beautiful local area of County Kerry.

Whether it's earrings, necklaces, bracelets or other pieces of jewellery - they are all made with care and precision to your own personal colour and design specifications

The materials I use in my production processes include crystal glass, electroplated glass, antique bronze, copper, silver plating, glass beads, pearls and more.

Please click or tap here for my contact form if you are interested in finding out more about my products or if you wish to get in touch with me about placing an order. I am also for available for exhibiting and selling at events, fairs and shows.
